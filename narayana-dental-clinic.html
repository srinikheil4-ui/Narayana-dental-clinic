FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

+
You can book online using the form on this page, or call us at 095919 61691. We also accept walk-ins subject to availability.

Do you treat children?

+
Yes! We offer dedicated paediatric dentistry for children from their first tooth onwards. Our doctors are trained to keep children calm and comfortable throughout treatment.

Is teeth whitening painful?

+
Professional teeth whitening at our clinic is generally comfortable. Some patients experience mild sensitivity during or after the procedure, which subsides within 24–48 hours.

How long does a root canal take?

+
Most root canal treatments at our clinic are completed in 1–2 sittings of 60–90 minutes each, depending on the complexity. Modern anaesthesia ensures a pain-free experience.

Do you offer dental implants?

+
Yes, we provide high-quality dental implants as a permanent tooth replacement solution. A consultation is required to assess suitability and create a personalised treatment plan.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

+
Call us immediately at 095919 61691. We offer same-day emergency care for toothaches, broken teeth, knocked-out teeth, and other urgent dental issues.

How often should I visit the dentist?

+
We recommend a dental check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months for most adults and children. Patients with specific conditions may need more frequent visits.

Are your prices reasonable?

+
We strive to provide high-quality care at fair, transparent prices. All charges are discussed with you before treatment begins. We do not believe in hidden fees or surprises.
